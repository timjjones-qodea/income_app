Imports

CSV files are staged and validated before anything reaches the portfolio ledger.

What to import

For each account, start with the AJ Bell portfolio export, then the AJ Bell cash statement. The AIC export is optional and is used as a regular-income planning baseline for investment trusts.

Full import guide

Recommended workflow

Complete the first two steps for each account. AIC is an optional refinement.

  1. 1
    AJ Bell portfolio · required first

    Creates the current holdings, quantities, tickers and market values.

  2. 2
    AJ Bell cash statement · actual income

    Use roughly the previous 12 months. Dividends, interest and payment-time quantities populate Income history.

  3. 3
    AIC portfolio · optional planning baseline

    Adds regular trailing income and helps keep special dividends out of the repeatable estimate.

Upload a CSV

AJ Bell portfolio, AJ Bell cash statement and AIC portfolio files require an account. The app will reject a cash statement until that account's portfolio has been committed.
