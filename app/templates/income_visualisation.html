Why these numbers can differ

AIC income is a current-holdings view: it says what the shares now held produced over the selected AIC period. Actual income is payment-date cash received in AJ Bell. Purchases, sales, missing cash statements and special dividends can make the two numbers diverge.

The app currently stores the exported AIC row values, but not the AIC screen options used to create the file. If you import both with and without special dividends, this page uses the latest committed AIC row for each account/security.
