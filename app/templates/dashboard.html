How to read this page

The dashboard separates two useful numbers: actual income received in the last 12 months, and planning income for current holdings. Planning income prefers AIC regular income where available so one-off special dividends do not quietly become “expected” income.

  1. Load AJ Bell portfolio CSVs for current holdings.
  2. Load AJ Bell cash statements for actual dividends and interest.
  3. Add AIC exports or manual assumptions to refine repeatable income.
